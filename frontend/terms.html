  • All payments are processed in Kenyan Shillings (KES)
  • Payment amounts are set by your landlord and cannot be altered by RentPortal
  • Once a payment is confirmed by Safaricom, it is final and non-refundable unless the landlord explicitly authorizes a refund
  • RentPortal is not responsible for failed transactions caused by insufficient M-Pesa balance, network issues or Safaricom service outages
  • Your M-Pesa PIN is never stored or seen by RentPortal — all PIN entry happens directly on Safaricom's secure platform
💡 Always verify the payment amount before entering your M-Pesa PIN. Contact your landlord immediately if you suspect an incorrect charge.
