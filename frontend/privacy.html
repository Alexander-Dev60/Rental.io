INTRODUCTION

RentPortal ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

This policy is compliant with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019. By using RentPortal, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

🔒 We never sell your personal data. Your information is only used to provide and improve our service.
02

DATA WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of personal data:

CategoryData CollectedWhen
IdentityFull name, email addressOn registration
ContactPhone number (for M-Pesa)On registration
FinancialPayment amounts, months, M-Pesa transaction codesOn payment
CommunicationIn-app messages between tenant and landlordWhen messages are sent
TechnicalLogin timestamps, session tokensOn login

We do not collect your M-Pesa PIN, national ID number, or bank account details.

03

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process rent payments via M-Pesa
  • To generate and email payment receipts
  • To send rent reminders and system notifications
  • To enable communication between tenants and landlords
  • To calculate and display arrears and payment history
  • To improve platform performance and fix bugs

We will never use your data for marketing, advertising or any purpose not listed above without your explicit consent.

04

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas cloud database hosted on AWS servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • Password encryption — all passwords are hashed using bcrypt (salt rounds: 10)
  • JWT authentication — sessions are protected by signed JSON Web Tokens
  • HTTPS only — all data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Access control — only authorized admins can access tenant data
  • Environment variables — all secrets are stored securely and never exposed in code
🛡️ Despite our best efforts, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong unique password and keep your credentials private.
05

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

RentPortal integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Safaricom M-Pesa — for payment processing. Their privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.
  • MongoDB Atlas — for database hosting. Data is stored in compliance with their data protection policies.
  • Resend / Brevo — for transactional email delivery (receipts, reminders).
  • Railway / Render — for backend hosting infrastructure.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

06

YOUR RIGHTS

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Right to object — object to how we process your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@rentportal.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

COOKIES

RentPortal uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your session token and theme preference on your device. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device — not sent to our servers except as part of API requests
  • Cleared when you sign out
  • Never used for advertising or tracking

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data.

08

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

RentPortal is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account.
