FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I pay rent through RentPortal? +
Go to your tenant dashboard, click "Pay Rent", enter the amount and month, then click "Pay with M-Pesa". You'll receive a prompt on your registered phone number. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and the payment is confirmed. A receipt is emailed to you automatically.
What happens if my M-Pesa payment fails? +
If a payment fails, it will show as "Failed" in your payment history. No money will be deducted from your account. You can try again immediately or contact your landlord through the dashboard chat for assistance.
Can I download my rent receipts? +
Yes! Every successful payment generates a PDF receipt that is emailed to you. You can also view and download all your receipts from the Receipts section in your tenant dashboard at any time.
How do I register as a tenant? +
Click "Get Started" and fill in your name, email, phone number and a password. Once registered, your landlord will assign you to a house unit. You'll receive a welcome email confirming your account.
Is my data safe on RentPortal? +
Absolutely. All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing. Your session is protected by JWT tokens and all API endpoints require authentication. We never store your M-Pesa PIN — payments are handled directly by Safaricom's secure servers.
I forgot my password. What do I do? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email. You'll receive a password reset link in your inbox. If you still have trouble, contact your landlord or reach out to our support team.
